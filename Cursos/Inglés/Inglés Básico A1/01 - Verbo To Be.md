# 01 — Verbo To Be (Ser / Estar)

---

## 📋 Conjugación

| Pronombre | To Be | Contracción |
|---|---|---|
| I | **am** | I'm |
| You | **are** | You're |
| He / She / It | **is** | He's / She's / It's |
| We | **are** | We're |
| You (plural) | **are** | You're |
| They | **are** | They're |

---

## ✅ Forma Afirmativa

```
I am a developer.       → I'm a developer.
She is from Colombia.   → She's from Colombia.
They are students.      → They're students.
```

---

## ❌ Forma Negativa

Agrega **not** después del verbo To Be:

```
I am not tired.        → I'm not tired.
He is not here.        → He's not here. / He isn't here.
We are not ready.      → We're not ready. / We aren't ready.
```

---

## ❓ Forma Interrogativa

Invierte el sujeto y el verbo:

```
Are you a programmer?    → Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
Is she from Medellín?   → Yes, she is. / No, she isn't.
Are they students?       → Yes, they are. / No, they aren't.
```

---

## 💡 Usos Principales

| Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|
| Identidad | I am Juan Diego |
| Origen | She is from Colombia |
| Ocupación | He is a developer |
| Características | The car is red |
| Estado | We are tired |
| Ubicación | They are at home |
