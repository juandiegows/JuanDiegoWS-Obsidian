# 02 — Presente Simple (Simple Present)

---

## 📋 Estructura

```
Sujeto + Verbo base (+ s/es en 3ª persona singular)
```

| Pronombre | Verbo "work" |
|---|---|
| I / You / We / They | work |
| He / She / It | work**s** |

---

## ✅ Forma Afirmativa

```
I work from home.
She works at Platzi.
They study English every day.
```

**Reglas para 3ª persona singular (he/she/it):**
- La mayoría: agrega **-s** → work → works
- Termina en -ch, -sh, -x, -o, -ss: agrega **-es** → watch → watches
- Termina en consonante + y: cambia **y → ies** → study → studies

---

## ❌ Forma Negativa

Usa **do not (don't)** o **does not (doesn't)** para he/she/it:

```
I don't like coffee.
She doesn't work on weekends.
They don't speak Spanish.
```

---

## ❓ Forma Interrogativa

```
Do you work from home?      → Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
Does she study every day?   → Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.
```

---

## 🕐 Cuándo Usar el Presente Simple

| Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|
| Rutinas / Hábitos | I go to the gym on Sundays 🏋️ |
| Hechos generales | Water boils at 100°C |
| Verdades permanentes | The sun rises in the east |
| Horarios fijos | The class starts at 7 PM |
